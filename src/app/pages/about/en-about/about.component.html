Mission

To be a global academic community where faith, knowledge, and practice come together to inspire transformed lives and more just, compassionate, and hopeful societies.

Vision

To train leaders with solid theological, spiritual, and academic preparation, capable of responding with wisdom and compassion to the challenges of our time. Through accessible and excellent online education, we promote critical thinking, intercultural dialogue, and a commitment to personal and community transformation.

Values
  1. Academic Excellence
    We seek deep, rigorous, and meaningful learning.
  2. Living Faith
    We promote a practical spirituality that strengthens everyday life.
  3. Inclusion
    We open our doors to all cultures, traditions, and beliefs.
  4. Compassion
    We form individuals who are sensitive to human suffering and committed to the well-being of others.
  5. Dialogue and Respect
    We value diversity as a source of growth and mutual understanding.
  6. Innovation
    We use online education as a bridge to accessible, dynamic, and transformative learning.
  7. Unwavering Integrity
    We act with honesty and transparency in every action, ensuring that our processes are free from ambiguity or improper practices.
  8. Integrity and Transparency
    We act consistently with what we believe, teach, and do, building trust through clear and authentic communication.
  9. Trust and Responsibility
    We cultivate relationships based on respect and shared responsibility, supporting each student on their journey of formation and growth.
  10. Academic Standards
    We uphold high standards in teaching and learning, offering rigorous, accessible, and transformative education.
  11. Ethical Commitment
    We encourage values that honor justice, solidarity, and the common good, creating an environment of integrity and sustainability.
  12. Care and Support
    We protect and value the dignity of each person, providing a safe, reliable, and enriching environment where every voice matters.
