The Master of Divinity program at World Theology University represents a high-level academic offering, designed to provide students with a profound theological understanding, a solid ethical foundation, and advanced practical competencies for ministerial leadership in both local and international contexts. Through a comprehensive and updated curriculum, this master's degree combines academic excellence, spiritual formation, and practical orientation, responding to the demands of leadership in a dynamic and multicultural world.

The program is structured to foster critical analysis of Scripture, rigorous study of Christian theology, applied ethics, Church history, and pastoral leadership. Students develop a robust theoretical foundation and strategic ministerial skills, preparing them to face contemporary challenges in Christian ministry, both within ecclesial settings and in broader societal contexts.

Thanks to its 100% online modality, the Master of Divinity program enables professionals from diverse regions and cultural backgrounds to access a high-quality education that is flexible and tailored to their needs. This structure promotes autonomous and collaborative learning, enriched by the diversity of international perspectives, thereby strengthening the intercultural dimension of ministerial leadership.

The formation is complemented by supervised practicums, the development of evaluation portfolios, and ministerial integration projects, allowing students to apply the knowledge acquired in real-world ministry settings, consolidating their spiritual, academic, and professional preparation.

This master's program is intended for:

  • Those aspiring to ministerial ordination or pastoral leadership within faith communities.
  • Professionals seeking to deepen their theological understanding to serve in educational, social, or community settings.
  • Students wishing to integrate faith and ministerial practice with a critical, ethical, and transformative approach.
  • Leaders in need of tools to respond to the ethical, social, and spiritual challenges of the twenty-first century.

To apply for the Master of Divinity program at World Theology University, candidates must meet the following academic and administrative requirements, ensuring access to a program of excellence and the continuation of international quality standards.

  • Hold a Bachelor's degree in Theology, Christian Ministry, or a related field from an accredited institution of higher education.
  • Submit an official academic transcript verifying the completion of the undergraduate degree.
  • Have a satisfactory academic record, equivalent to a minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale, or its equivalent in the applicant’s educational system.
  • Submit a statement of purpose outlining the applicant’s interest in pursuing the Master's degree and their ministerial or professional goals.
  • Provide two letters of recommendation, either academic or ministerial, attesting to the applicant’s training, integrity, and vocational commitment.
  • Possess basic competencies in the use of digital platforms for distance education.
  • Have reliable internet access and an appropriate device for online study.
  • Complete the application form and submit all required documentation within the established deadlines.

World Theology University evaluates each application comprehensively, considering both academic merit and the applicant’s vocation for service and ethical commitment. The admissions process seeks to ensure that each student is fully prepared to benefit from and contribute to the academic and formative environment of the Master of Divinity program.
