This course offers advanced training in transformational leadership and strategic management applied to ministries and religious organizations. Students will develop ethical, strategic, and practical competencies to lead with vision, innovation, and commitment in dynamic and diverse contexts. The course integrates theological principles, managerial skills, and contemporary leadership approaches, preparing leaders capable of driving positive change and sustainability within their ministerial communities.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the theoretical and practical foundations of transformational leadership.
  • Apply ethical and strategic principles in ministerial and organizational management.
  • Develop effective leadership skills for contexts of change and cultural diversity.
  • Design strategic plans for the growth and sustainability of ministries and organizations.
  • Promote innovation and a transformative vision in ministerial action.
  • Integrate principles of people, resource, and project management with a theological and ethical perspective.

Main Topics

  • Foundations of transformational leadership in ministerial contexts.
  • Contemporary models of ethical and strategic leadership.
  • Design and implementation of strategic plans in religious organizations.
  • Change management, innovation, and conflict resolution in ministries.
  • Management of human, financial, and technological resources in ministry settings.
  • Building high-performance teams and fostering collaborative leadership.
  • Ethics of Christian leadership in a globalized world.
  • Practical cases of transformational leadership in churches and religious organizations.
