This course provides specialized, continuous, and personalized support throughout the research and writing process of the doctoral thesis. Under the supervision of an assigned thesis advisor, students will advance in the development of original research projects that make significant contributions to the field of theology. The course is designed to strengthen methodological rigor, argumentative coherence, and contextual relevance of the thesis work, ensuring high academic standards and a global impact.

Course Objectives

  • Consolidate autonomous and advanced research skills in the theological field.
  • Apply rigorous and pertinent methodologies in the development of the doctoral thesis.
  • Critically integrate relevant and up-to-date bibliographic sources.
  • Strengthen academic writing and the logical structuring of the doctoral text.
  • Refine theological argumentation skills in dialogue with contemporary contexts.
  • Prepare a solid, critical, and coherent oral defense of research findings.

Main Topics

  • Advanced planning of doctoral research.
  • Personalized supervision and guidance throughout the writing process.
  • Critical analysis of findings and methodological adjustments.
  • Application of international citation standards and academic writing styles.
  • Ethics in research and responsible information management.
  • Preparation of thesis chapters: structure, coherence, and consistency.
  • Presentation of findings and relevant theological conclusions.
  • Doctoral defense simulation and academic feedback.
