MAMH-PCEI-500 - Professional Guidance and Ethical Issues in Mental Health Counseling

The course MAMH-PCEI-500 - Professional Guidance and Ethical Issues in Mental Health Counseling offers an in-depth view of the history and ethical principles governing the mental health counseling profession. Topics include professional roles, licenses, and certifications, as well as the application of ethics in decision-making and intervention during crisis situations.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the history and evolution of the mental health counseling profession.
  • Identify the fundamental ethical principles applied in counseling.
  • Examine legal and professional requirements, such as licenses and certifications.
  • Analyze ethical dilemmas and develop strategies for responsible decision-making.
  • Explore ethical intervention in crises and emergency situations.

Main Topics:

  • History and development of mental health counseling.
  • Ethical principles and professional codes of conduct.
  • Licenses, certifications, and legal regulations.
  • Ethics applied to decision-making in counseling.
  • Crisis intervention and ethical considerations.
  • Case studies on ethical dilemmas in professional practice.

Methodology:

The course is developed through reading analysis, case studies, forum discussions, and ethical decision-making simulations. Critical thinking and the application of ethical principles in real-life scenarios are encouraged.

Assessment:

Course assessment will include:

  • Essays on the history and regulation of mental health counseling.
  • Case studies on ethical dilemmas in professional practice.
  • Debates on ethical principles and their application in decision-making.
  • Written reflections on crisis intervention experiences.
  • Theoretical evaluations on the fundamentals of ethics in counseling.

Importance of the Course:

Ethics and professional regulation are fundamental pillars in mental health counseling. This course provides the knowledge and tools necessary to practice the profession responsibly, ensuring client well-being and compliance with ethical and legal standards.
