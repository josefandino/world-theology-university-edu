MATH-CPR-503 - Church Planting and Renewal

Church Planting and Renewal is a course designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to plant new churches and renew those facing challenges. Through this course, students will learn the key principles behind church planting, including establishing a vision, building a faith community, and designing effective strategies for growth and sustainability. The course will also address aspects related to the renewal of existing churches, including how to revitalize stagnant congregations and restore focus on the church's mission.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the fundamental principles of church planting and key strategies for effectiveness.
  • Learn how to design a church planting plan, from initial vision to community establishment.
  • Develop pastoral leadership skills that foster church renewal and growth.
  • Explore methods for revitalizing existing churches and fostering a renewed focus on mission.
  • Identify common challenges in church planting and how to successfully overcome them.

Main Topics:

  • Foundations of church planting: vision, mission, and strategy.
  • Developing and forming healthy Christian communities.
  • Practical aspects of church establishment: logistics, finances, and structure.
  • Pastoral leadership in the context of a new church and in the renewal of existing churches.
  • Strategies for revitalizing stagnant churches: from restructuring to spiritual restoration.
  • The impact of church planting on the local community and cultural context.

Methodology:

This course will combine theoretical classes with case studies, simulations, and practical church planting planning. Students will be challenged to develop real-life projects for planting new churches or renewing existing churches, evaluating each stage of these processes. There will also be opportunities to discuss leadership challenges and learn to apply successful strategies for long-term growth and sustainability.

Assessment:

  • Development of a church planting or renewal plan, focusing on vision, mission, and strategy.
  • Case studies on church planting and congregation renewal.
  • Presentation of practical projects with feasibility analysis and growth strategies.

Importance of the Course:

This course is essential for those wishing to undertake the task of planting new churches or revitalizing existing congregations. Church planting and renewal are key processes for the growth of God's Kingdom, and this course prepares students to lead with wisdom and effectiveness in these contexts. By learning to develop sustainable and healthy churches, students will be able to positively impact the communities they serve.
