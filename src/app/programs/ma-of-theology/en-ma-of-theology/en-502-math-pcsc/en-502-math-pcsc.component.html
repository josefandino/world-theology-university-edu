MATH-PCSC-502 - Pastoral Care and Spiritual Counseling

Pastoral Care and Spiritual Counseling is a course designed to equip students with the essential practices of pastoral care and spiritual counseling. Through this course, students will learn how to provide emotional, spiritual, and psychological support to individuals in various life situations, including crises, family issues, illnesses, and other challenging circumstances. The course will also cover ethical principles and best practices for helping church members grow spiritually and face personal and communal challenges.

Course Objectives:

  • Develop spiritual counseling skills based on biblical principles and pastoral sensitivity.
  • Learn how to provide emotional and spiritual support to those facing various crises and difficulties.
  • Establish solid ethical principles for the practice of pastoral counseling.
  • Equip students to offer spiritual care to people from diverse contexts, fostering their holistic well-being.
  • Explore the psycho-spiritual aspects of pastoral care and the importance of a holistic approach in counseling.

Main Topics:

  • The role of the pastor as counselor: principles and functions.
  • Spiritual accompaniment in times of crisis: grief, serious illness, divorce, etc.
  • Active listening and empathy in spiritual counseling.
  • Pastoral intervention in family and personal conflicts.
  • Ethics and boundaries in pastoral counseling practice.

Methodology:

This course will combine theoretical classes with practical exercises, case studies, and counseling simulations to help students develop the necessary skills for pastoral care. Students will have opportunities to practice counseling sessions in a controlled environment, receiving feedback and guidance from experienced professionals. Assessment:

  • Essays on spiritual counseling theories and practices.
  • Pastoral counseling simulations with feedback.
  • Case studies on crisis situations and how to approach them from a pastoral perspective.

Importance of the Course:

This course is essential for those who wish to play an active role in the spiritual and emotional care of church members. Pastoral care and spiritual counseling are fundamental to the holistic well-being of believers and the church community. Students will learn to become spiritual guides in times of crisis, helping people find comfort, direction, and hope through their faith.
