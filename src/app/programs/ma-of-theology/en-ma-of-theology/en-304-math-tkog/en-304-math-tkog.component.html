MATH-TKOG-304 - Theology of the Kingdom of God

Theology of the Kingdom of God is a course that explores the central concept of the Kingdom of God in Christian theology, its meaning, manifestation, and realization throughout Scripture. Students will study how the Kingdom of God is presented in the gospels, epistles, the Old Testament, and the redemptive work of Jesus Christ. The course addresses the nature of the Kingdom, its relationship to the church, salvation, and how it should be lived by believers today.

Course Objectives:

  • Study the doctrine of the Kingdom of God throughout Scripture, from the Old Testament to the New Testament.
  • Analyze Jesus’ teachings about the Kingdom of God, particularly in the gospels.
  • Explore the relationship between the Kingdom of God, the church, and salvation in Christ’s redemptive work.
  • Reflect on the Kingdom of God as both a present and future reality for Christians.
  • Investigate how Christians are to live according to the values of the Kingdom of God in their daily lives.

Main Topics:

  • The concept of the Kingdom of God in the Old Testament.
  • Jesus’ preaching on the Kingdom of God in the gospels.
  • The relationship between the Kingdom of God and the church.
  • The Kingdom of God and eschatology: the already and the not yet.
  • Ethical and spiritual implications of living according to the values of the Kingdom of God.
  • The Kingdom of God in Christian mission and its relevance for the modern church.

Methodology:

The course will be conducted through theoretical lectures, analysis of key biblical passages, theological discussions, and case studies. Students will also engage in research on the relationship between the theology of the Kingdom and its application in contemporary Christian life.

Assessment:

  • Essays on the theology of the Kingdom of God in different biblical contexts.
  • Written exams on Jesus’ teachings about the Kingdom.
  • Presentations on the practical implications of the Kingdom of God for the church.
  • Research on the eschatology of the Kingdom of God and its relevance to Christian life.

Importance of the Course:

This course is essential for understanding the concept of the Kingdom of God, which is central to Jesus' teachings and Christian theology as a whole. Understanding the Kingdom of God enables believers to live according to Kingdom principles in their daily lives and engage in God's mission in the world.
