BATHE-PS-1400 - Practical Spirituality

This course fosters the development and strengthening of spiritual disciplines such as prayer, fasting, Bible reading, and other devotional practices. It aims to consciously and consistently integrate these disciplines into daily life, promoting a deep, balanced, and sustainable spirituality that impacts personal, communal, and ministerial life.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the biblical and theological foundations of spiritual disciplines.
  • Analyze the importance of practical spirituality in holistic Christian growth.
  • Develop consistent spiritual habits applicable to daily life.
  • Explore various expressions of spiritual disciplines throughout Christian history.
  • Evaluate the benefits and challenges of integrating spiritual practices in contemporary contexts.
  • Apply spiritual disciplines to strengthen personal, communal, and ministerial life.

Main Topics:

  • Biblical foundations of Christian spirituality.
  • Prayer: constant communion with God.
  • Fasting: a discipline of dependence and spiritual renewal.
  • Reading and meditating on Scripture: formation through the Word.
  • Silence, solitude, and contemplation: spaces for spiritual encounter.
  • Community discipline: spiritual life within the Christian community.
  • Spirituality and daily life: practical and coherent integration.
  • Challenges to spirituality in the contemporary world.

Methodology:

The course will combine expository virtual classes, guided practice of spiritual disciplines, personal reflection journals, experience-sharing forums, and spiritual integration projects. Experiential learning, spiritual mentoring, and the development of a personal spiritual growth plan will be emphasized.

Assessment:

  • Reflection essays on the meaning and practice of spiritual disciplines.
  • Personal journal documenting weekly spiritual practices.
  • Active participation in spiritual workshops and discussion forums.
  • Self-assessments of spiritual growth throughout the course.
  • Final project: design of a personal and communal spiritual life plan.

Course Significance:

Practical Spirituality is essential for forming leaders and believers who live out an authentic, deep, and transformative faith. This course encourages the integration of spiritual disciplines into daily life, strengthening Christian character, renewing personal and communal mission, and promoting a solid and sustainable spirituality amid the challenges of the contemporary world.
