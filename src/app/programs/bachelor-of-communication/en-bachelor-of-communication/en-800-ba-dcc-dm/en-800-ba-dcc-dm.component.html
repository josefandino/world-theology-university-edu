Principles and tools of digital marketing, social media, SEO, SEM, and data analysis.

The course Digital Marketing provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles, strategies, and essential tools for marketing in digital environments. Topics include social media management, search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), and data analytics for decision-making. Students will acquire key competencies to design, execute, and evaluate digital marketing campaigns tailored to dynamic and global audiences.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the basic concepts and evolution of digital marketing.
  • Apply effective marketing strategies on social media platforms.
  • Implement SEO techniques to improve search engine visibility.
  • Design digital advertising campaigns using SEM tools.
  • Analyze digital data to evaluate campaign performance and optimize results.
  • Develop ethical, inclusive, and culturally adapted marketing strategies.

Main Topics:

  • Introduction to digital marketing: key concepts and current trends.
  • Social media management: strategy, planning, and content analysis.
  • SEO fundamentals: on-page and off-page optimization.
  • SEM: Google Ads and social media advertising campaigns.
  • Content marketing and digital branding.
  • Digital analytics tools: Google Analytics and key metrics.
  • Automation strategies and audience segmentation.
  • Ethical and legal considerations in digital marketing.

Methodology:

The course will be delivered virtually, integrating theory and practice through:

  • Live virtual classes on digital strategies and tools.
  • Case studies of successful campaigns and common mistakes.
  • Hands-on SEO and SEM implementation in simulated environments.
  • Projects for the design and management of real or simulated campaigns.
  • Discussion forums on trends and best practices in digital marketing.
  • Guided readings on innovation and digital transformation in marketing.

Assessment:

  • Development of digital marketing campaigns.
  • Website optimization using SEO techniques.
  • Analysis and reporting of digital campaign metrics.
  • Active participation in forums and collaborative activities.
  • Final project: comprehensive design of a digital marketing strategy.

Course Significance:

Digital Marketing is essential for preparing professionals to lead communication and marketing strategies in a highly connected and competitive world. Through this course, students will acquire practical and strategic skills to create effective campaigns, adapt to the dynamics of the digital environment, and contribute ethically and innovatively to the growth of organizations in the global market.
