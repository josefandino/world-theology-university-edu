BA-DCC-SMMS-2400 – Social Media Marketing Strategies

Techniques for managing and promoting brands and products through social media platforms.

The course Social Media Marketing Strategies provides both theoretical foundations and practical tools for managing and promoting brands, products, and services across major social media platforms. Students will learn to design content strategies, segment audiences, manage both paid and organic campaigns, and analyze performance metrics, applying best practices that respond to the evolving digital ecosystem.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the strategic role of social media in digital marketing.
  • Design content strategies tailored to various social media platforms.
  • Apply audience segmentation techniques and manage campaigns on social platforms.
  • Use analytics tools to evaluate campaign performance.
  • Manage brand identity and reputation across social networks.
  • Promote ethical, creative, and effective communication in digital environments.

Main Topics:

  • Introduction to social media marketing: principles and evolution.
  • Main platforms: Facebook, Instagram, X (formerly Twitter), LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube.
  • Content strategies: storytelling, engagement, and community management.
  • Paid campaigns: Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads.
  • Advanced segmentation and message personalization.
  • Social media monitoring and online crisis management.
  • Analytics tools and key performance metrics for social media.
  • Best ethical and legal practices in social media management.

Methodology:

The course may be delivered in virtual, online, or hybrid (virtual-online) format, combining theory, practice, and critical analysis through:

  • Dynamic virtual classes on social media management.
  • Workshops on the design of strategies and content for social platforms.
  • Simulations of campaigns and crisis management on social media.
  • Case studies on successful campaigns and analysis of digital trends.
  • Discussion forums on innovation, responsibility, and digital communication.
  • Final project: development of a comprehensive social media marketing strategy.

Assessment:

  • Design of campaigns and content production for social media platforms.
  • Active participation in workshops, simulations, and discussion forums.
  • Analysis of performance metrics from social media campaigns.
  • Final integrative project: social media marketing strategy.
