BA-DCC-ADA-2200 – Advanced Digital Advertising

Design of advertising campaigns on advanced digital platforms such as Google Ads, Facebook Ads, among others.

The course Advanced Digital Advertising provides specialized training in the design, execution, and optimization of advertising campaigns on leading digital platforms such as Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram, LinkedIn, and others. Students will learn to use advanced segmentation, analytics, and automation tools to maximize reach, conversion, and return on investment (ROI) in highly competitive digital environments.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the landscape of advanced digital advertising and its main platforms.
  • Design strategic advertising campaigns tailored to various audiences and objectives.
  • Apply segmentation, optimization, and remarketing techniques in digital campaigns.
  • Use digital analytics tools to measure and improve advertising performance.
  • Manage budgets, bidding strategies, and conversions on digital ad platforms.
  • Promote ethical, responsible, and culturally inclusive advertising practices.

Main Topics:

  • Programmatic advertising and the digital ad ecosystem.
  • Google Ads: search, display, video, and shopping campaigns.
  • Facebook Ads and Meta Business Suite: campaign design and management on social media.
  • Advanced segmentation and retargeting strategies.
  • Campaign automation and the use of artificial intelligence in advertising.
  • Conversion optimization and sales funnel management.
  • Advertising analytics: interpreting key metrics and reporting.
  • Ethical and regulatory aspects of digital advertising.

Methodology:

The course may be delivered in virtual, online, or hybrid (virtual-online) format, combining theory, practice, and simulation through:

  • Virtual lectures on strategies and platforms for advanced digital advertising.
  • Workshops on the creation and optimization of real or simulated campaigns.
  • Case studies on successful campaigns and common pitfalls in digital advertising.
  • Discussion forums on innovation, trends, and ethics in digital advertising.
  • Projects involving campaign implementation on specific platforms.
  • Guided readings on best practices and technological advances in digital marketing.

Assessment:

  • Design, execution, and optimization of digital campaigns.
  • Interpretation of metrics and development of performance reports.
  • Active participation in workshops, simulations, and discussion forums.
  • Final integrative project: advanced digital advertising strategy.

Course Significance:

Advanced Digital Advertising is essential for training professionals capable of leading high-impact campaigns in today’s competitive digital landscape. This course equips students with the technical and strategic skills needed to maximize results, adapt to new technologies, and act ethically and effectively in the global digital advertising market.
