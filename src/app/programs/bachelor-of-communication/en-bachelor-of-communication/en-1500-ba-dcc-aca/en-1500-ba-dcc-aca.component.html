Advertising strategies and campaign development across various media platforms.

The course Advertising and Creative Advertising introduces students to the strategic and creative foundations of contemporary advertising. It examines the stages of advertising campaign development, the principles of persuasive communication, and creative techniques applied to both traditional and digital media. The course promotes innovation, strategic thinking, and cultural awareness as essential elements in crafting effective and ethically responsible advertising messages.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the principles and strategies of modern advertising.
  • Develop skills to create persuasive and creative advertising messages.
  • Apply planning and execution techniques for advertising campaigns.
  • Critically analyze advertising campaigns across cultural and media contexts.
  • Use both digital and traditional tools to distribute advertising content.
  • Promote ethical, inclusive, and socially responsible advertising practices.

Main Topics:

  • History and evolution of advertising.
  • Planning processes in advertising campaigns.
  • Persuasive communication strategies and message construction.
  • Creative advertising: concepts, techniques, and processes.
  • Advertising in traditional media: print, radio, and television.
  • Digital advertising: social media, content marketing, and online campaigns.
  • Measuring effectiveness and evaluating advertising campaigns.
  • Ethics, social responsibility, and cultural diversity in advertising.

Methodology:

The course may be delivered in virtual, online, or hybrid format, combining theory, analysis, and practical application through:

  • Virtual classes on advertising strategies and creative techniques.
  • Hands-on workshops for developing advertising materials.
  • Case studies of successful and failed advertising campaigns.
  • Simulations for designing campaigns across various media and audiences.
  • Discussion forums on trends, innovation, and advertising ethics.
  • Final project: planning and development of an integrated advertising campaign.

Assessment:

  • Design and presentation of individual and group advertising pieces.
  • Critical analysis of real-world advertising campaigns.
  • Active participation in workshops, simulations, and discussion forums.
  • Final project: creation and planning of a complete advertising campaign.

Course Significance:

Advertising and Creative Advertising is essential for training professionals capable of conceiving and executing innovative, effective, and ethically responsible advertising campaigns in a highly competitive global market. This course develops strategic thinking, message creation, and media application skills to connect effectively with diverse audiences in dynamic cultural contexts.
