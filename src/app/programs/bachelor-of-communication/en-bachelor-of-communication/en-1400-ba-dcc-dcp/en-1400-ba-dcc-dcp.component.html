BA-DCC-DCP-1400 – Digital Content Production

Creation of content for blogs, podcasts, YouTube channels, and streaming platforms.

The course Digital Content Production trains students in the creation, production, and management of content for various digital platforms. Through a practical and creative approach, students will explore digital communication strategies, writing techniques, audiovisual production, and audience management across blogs, podcasts, YouTube channels, and streaming services. The course encourages innovation and adaptability to the current trends of the digital ecosystem.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the digital content ecosystem and its main platforms.
  • Develop skills in writing, audio, and audiovisual content creation.
  • Apply communication and digital marketing strategies in content production.
  • Use text, audio, and video editing tools to optimize content quality.
  • Effectively manage digital communities and audiences.
  • Promote ethical, creative, and sustainable practices in digital production.

Main Topics:

  • Overview of digital platforms: blogs, podcasts, YouTube, streaming.
  • Content strategies: digital storytelling and audience engagement.
  • Blog production: SEO writing and content optimization.
  • Podcast production: scripting, recording, editing, and publishing.
  • Video content creation for YouTube and video platforms.
  • Use of digital tools for audio, video, and graphic design editing.
  • Digital community management and performance metrics analysis.
  • Ethical and legal aspects in digital content production.

Methodology:

The course may be delivered in virtual, online, or hybrid format, integrating theory, practice, and creativity through:

  • Interactive virtual classes on production and digital trends.
  • Workshops on blog, podcast, and video creation.
  • Content projects for real or simulated platforms.
  • Critical analysis of successful digital content campaigns.
  • Forums on innovation and digital transformation in communication.
  • Guided readings on content production and marketing strategies.

Assessment:

  • Production of SEO-optimized blogs for web platforms.
  • Production and publishing of thematic podcasts.
  • Creation of audiovisual content for YouTube or video platforms.
  • Active participation in workshops and discussion forums.
  • Final integrative project: launch of a digital content channel or series.

Course Significance:

Digital Content Production is essential for training communicators capable of acting with creativity, strategy, and responsibility in today’s digital environment. The ability to create and manage relevant content enables professionals to position themselves competitively, connect with diverse audiences, and lead communication and innovation initiatives across global digital platforms.
