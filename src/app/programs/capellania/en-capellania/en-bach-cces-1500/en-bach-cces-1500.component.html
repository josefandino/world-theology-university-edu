BACH-CCES-1500 - Crisis and chaplaincy in emergency situations

The course BACH-CCES-1500 - Crisis and Chaplaincy in Emergency Situations provides specialized training for chaplains and spiritual leaders in crisis and emergency care in various settings, both religious and civil. Through theological and psychological principles, students will acquire the skills to intervene in moments of disaster, trauma, and critical situations, offering pastoral and emotional support to affected individuals.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the role of the chaplain in emergency and crisis situations.
  • Develop intervention skills in natural disasters, conflicts, and personal crises.
  • Apply biblical and psychological principles in assisting victims and survivors.
  • Strengthen active listening skills and pastoral accompaniment in crisis contexts.
  • Learn strategies for coordinating with emergency response organizations.

Main Topics:

  • Foundations of chaplaincy in emergencies and crises.
  • Managing stress and trauma in victims and rescuers.
  • Intervention strategies and emotional first aid.
  • Spiritual counseling in situations of grief and loss.
  • Interdisciplinary work with emergency response teams.
  • Ethics and confidentiality in pastoral crisis care.
  • Case studies and real-life experiences in emergency care.

Methodology:

The course combines theoretical study with practical experience through simulations, real case analysis, and crisis intervention exercises. It includes virtual lectures, forum debates, and the development of action plans for emergency scenarios.

Evaluation:

The evaluation will be carried out through:

  • Theoretical exams on crisis intervention principles.
  • Analysis of case studies and application of counseling strategies.
  • Participation in emergency care simulations.
  • Development of an action plan for a specific crisis.
  • Written reflections on the chaplain's role in critical situations.

Importance of the Course:

In times of crisis and emergency, the presence of a well-prepared chaplain can make a difference in the lives of those affected. This course provides the necessary tools to act with compassion, wisdom, and effectiveness in times of difficulty, promoting resilience and hope in crisis-affected communities.
