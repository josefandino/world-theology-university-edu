BACH-APC-1400 - Advanced Pastoral Counseling

The course BACH-APC-1400 - Advanced Pastoral Counseling is designed to equip students with the skills necessary to address complex situations within chaplaincy ministry. Through the study of theoretical approaches and advanced counseling techniques, participants will learn to provide spiritual and emotional support to individuals facing personal crises, trauma, family conflict, and psychological challenges.

Course Objectives:

  • Develop specialized skills in pastoral counseling for handling difficult situations.
  • Understand the psychological and theological principles that support effective counseling.
  • Learn strategies for managing crises, grief, depression, and interpersonal conflict.
  • Apply biblical and therapeutic models in spiritual accompaniment.
  • Strengthen active listening and empathy skills in the counseling process.

Main Topics:

  • Biblical and theological foundations of pastoral counseling.
  • Psychological approaches applied to Christian counseling.
  • Techniques for managing grief and loss.
  • Crisis intervention and emotional first aid.
  • Counseling in cases of anxiety, depression, and trauma.
  • Ethics and confidentiality in pastoral counseling.
  • Family counseling and relationship restoration.

Methodology:

The course will be delivered through theoretical sessions, case analysis, counseling simulations, and Bible studies applied to pastoral practice. Students will engage in discussions, role-playing exercises, and skills training workshops focused on counseling techniques.

Assessment:

Course evaluation will include:

  • Case studies and analysis of real-life scenarios.
  • Participation in counseling session simulations.
  • Reflective essays on ethical principles in counseling.
  • Theoretical exams on the foundations of pastoral counseling.
  • A final project in which students develop a counseling plan for a specific situation.

Course Relevance:

In a world where individuals face numerous emotional and spiritual challenges, advanced pastoral counseling becomes an essential tool for chaplains and ministry leaders. This course provides the necessary competencies to guide, support, and restore those in need of direction during times of crisis, strengthening their faith and emotional well-being.
